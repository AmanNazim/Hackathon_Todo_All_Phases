## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Registration

Visit [https://todoapp.com/register](https://todoapp.com/register)

### Step 2: Fill in Your Information

Provide the following information:
- **Email Address:** Your valid email address
- **Password:** A secure password (see requirements below)
- **First Name:** Your first name
- **Last Name:** Your last name

### Step 3: Password Requirements

Your password must meet these requirements:
- âœ“ At least 8 characters long
- âœ“ Contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- âœ“ Contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- âœ“ Contains at least one number (0-9)
- âœ“ Contains at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Examples of strong passwords:**
- `MySecure@Pass2024`
- `Todo!List#2024`
- `Productive&Safe99`

**Avoid:**
- Common passwords like "Password123!"
- Personal information (birthdays, names)
- Sequential characters (12345, abcde)

### Step 4: Submit Registration

Click the "Create Account" button. You'll receive a confirmation message and a verification email.

### Step 5: Verify Your Email

Check your email inbox for a verification message from Todo App. Click the verification link to activate your account.

**Note:** The verification link expires after 24 hours.

## Logging In

### Step 1: Navigate to Login

Visit [https://todoapp.com/login](https://todoapp.com/login)

### Step 2: Enter Credentials

- **Email:** Your registered email address
- **Password:** Your account password

### Step 3: Click "Log In"

You'll be redirected to your dashboard upon successful login.

### Security Features

**Account Lockout Protection:**
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 15 minutes
- This protects your account from unauthorized access attempts

**Session Duration:**
- You'll stay logged in for 7 days
- After 7 days, you'll need to log in again

## Email Verification

### Why Verify Your Email?

Email verification ensures:
- You own the email address
- You can receive important notifications
- You can reset your password if needed

### Verification Process

1. **Check Your Inbox:** Look for an email from noreply@todoapp.com
2. **Click the Link:** Click the "Verify Email" button in the email
3. **Confirmation:** You'll see a success message

### Didn't Receive the Email?

**Check These First:**
- Spam/Junk folder
- Promotions tab (Gmail)
- Email address spelling

**Resend Verification Email:**
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your profile
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Check your inbox again

**Still Having Issues?**
Contact support at support@todoapp.com

## Resetting Your Password

### When to Reset

Reset your password if you:
- Forgot your current password
- Suspect unauthorized access
- Want to update to a more secure password

### Reset Process

**Step 1: Request Reset**
1. Go to [https://todoapp.com/forgot-password](https://todoapp.com/forgot-password)
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"

**Step 2: Check Your Email**
- You'll receive a password reset email within a few minutes
- The reset link expires after 1 hour

**Step 3: Create New Password**
1. Click the reset link in your email
2. Enter your new password (must meet requirements)
3. Confirm your new password
4. Click "Reset Password"

**Step 4: Log In**
- Use your new password to log in
- Your old password will no longer work

### Security Notes

- Reset links can only be used once
- If you didn't request a reset, you can safely ignore the email
- Your account remains secure even if someone requests a reset

## Changing Your Password

### When Logged In

If you're already logged in and want to change your password:

**Step 1: Go to Settings**
1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select "Settings"
3. Click "Security" tab

**Step 2: Change Password**
1. Enter your current password
2. Enter your new password
3. Confirm your new password
4. Click "Update Password"

**Step 3: Confirmation**
- You'll receive a confirmation email
- You'll remain logged in on your current device
- Other devices will need to log in again

### Requirements

- You must know your current password
- Your email must be verified
- New password must meet security requirements

## Account Security

### Best Practices

**1. Use a Strong Password**
- Unique to Todo App (don't reuse passwords)
- Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- At least 12 characters recommended

**2. Keep Your Email Secure**
- Use a strong email password
- Enable two-factor authentication on your email
- Don't share your email credentials

**3. Be Cautious of Phishing**
- Todo App will never ask for your password via email
- Always check the URL before entering credentials
- Look for https:// and the lock icon

**4. Log Out on Shared Devices**
- Always log out when using public computers
- Don't save passwords on shared devices

**5. Monitor Your Account**
- Review your account activity regularly
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** "Invalid email or password"

**Solutions:**
1. Check your email spelling
2. Ensure Caps Lock is off
3. Try resetting your password
4. Clear browser cache and cookies

---

**Problem:** "Account temporarily locked"

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 15 minutes and try again
2. Reset your password if you forgot it
3. Contact support if you believe this is an error

---

### Email Issues

**Problem:** Not receiving emails

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@todoapp.com to contacts
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Try a different email provider
5. Contact support

---

**Problem:** Verification link expired

**Solutions:**
1. Request a new verification email
2. Log in and click "Resend Verification"
3. Check email within 24 hours

---

### Password Reset Issues

**Problem:** Reset link doesn't work

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're using the latest reset email
2. Copy and paste the full URL
3. Request a new reset link
4. Try a different browser

---

**Problem:** "Token expired" error

**Solutions:**
1. Request a new password reset
2. Complete the reset within 1 hour
3. Check your email immediately after requesting

---

### Account Access

**Problem:** Can't access account

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Try password reset
3. Check if account is locked
4. Contact support with account details

---

**Problem:** Account deactivated

**Solutions:**
1. Contact support at support@todoapp.com
2. Provide your email address
3. Explain the situation
4. Wait for support team response
